This article explores three ways AI is transforming packaging: administrative efficiency, where LLMs handle tedious tasks like order processing and delivery coordination; creative enhancement, where AI acts as a brainstorming partner generating unconventional ideas without replacing designers; and material optimization, where AI analyzes data to optimize structural design and material thickness, reducing costs and environmental impact at scale.
