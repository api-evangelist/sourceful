Sourceful earned ISO/IEC 42001:2023 certification, the world's first standard for AI Management Systems, covering responsible AI across design (bias and IP review), training (data only from consenting partners with versioned, auditable datasets), and deployment (outputs include generating prompts and prior context for full traceability). The company describes its approach as domain-first, informed by 10k+ real packaging briefs and evaluated by human structural engineers and designers, positioning the certification as alignment with emerging EU AI Act requirements rolling out through 2026.
