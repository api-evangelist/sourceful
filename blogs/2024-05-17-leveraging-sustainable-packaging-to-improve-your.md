Citing a 2023 McKinsey report that around 90% of shoppers consider the environmental impact of packaging important, this article explains how sustainable packaging can improve brand image. It encourages brands to adopt recycled or biodegradable materials that support circular economy principles, reduce waste, and potentially lower supply chain emissions.
